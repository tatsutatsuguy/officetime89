• Meet RAITA's Staff •

Cathy

Say hello to our personable receptionist, Cathy. She’s a humorous and warm person. A perfect fit for RAITA’s front desk. If you need any guidance, she can point you in the right direction.

G.G.Greg

Meet our software department lead, and your immediate supervisor. G.G.Greg can help you out if you have any job questions. He will also be your trainer, so make sure you listen to the advice he has to give.

Cindy

Aside from you, Cindy is our most recent hire. She is a junior developer that shares an office with you. For projects to be successful you’ll need to communicate well with each other. We hope you get along!

Paige

Paige is our accounts officer. She works reports to our office’s program director. She is very busy, but she’ll be able to help you with any fiscal questions. She also shares an office with our graphic designer.

Pauline

Say “hello” to our resident graphic designer. Pauline can be a bit playful and aloof sometimes, but she is a hard worker, and it shows in her work. She’s always willing to hear your advice for projects, so feel free to approach her.

Sarah

Sarah is our Program Director. She is often overwhelmed by all the workload, and might seem a bit short with staff sometimes, but she is one of our most important staff. Please show your support, and she and RAITA will reciprocate.



• An Entire City to Explore! •

The Office

RAITA is a software company that you have found yourself recently hired at. You can go to work there on weekdays.

Make sure you don't wonder around town too much in the morning or you might be late to the office. Work hard to obtain more money, or spend your time socializing with your coworkers to build and make new relationships.

Tips:
Seems like a certain overly ambitious coworker has been working on weekends. It might help your career to investigate on the weekends. It may even help advance your relationship with someone too!

Out on the Town

There are plenty of places to go, and sites to see all over town. Stop in for a coffee or get a refreshing workout after hours at the gym. There is also a Mall downtown that is full of shops and eateries for it’s customers. Town life offers a variety of places to go on dates, or just socialize. Make sure you are getting the most out of your weekends by visiting all the shops!

Ordering food will give you energy and and going places like the barber or the gym will increase your attractiveness. Everything cost money though, so make sure you’re working hard enough to earn all the perks.

Tips:
Your coworkers all live in the same town. Try visiting various places in the morning before work and maybe you spend some time walking to work together.

Apartment Life

Your new apartment might be small, but it’s also cozy. Within your apartment you’ll find all the amenities needed for rest and leisure time. You can reach out to your friends on your phone, order food, listen to music and of course get a good night’s sleep so you have plenty of energy for a productive day at the office

Tips:
If you’re feeling drained try giving your mother a call. Sometimes a pep talk from a loved one can really motivate you.
